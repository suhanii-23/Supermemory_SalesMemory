PROPOSAL DISCUSSION — TCO CHALLENGE RESPONSE
Date: June 3, 2026
Related: Greg Simmons' pushback on build-cost assumptions

NVIDIA's finance team returned a materially lower internal build estimate (0.75 FTE-year vs. MemoryIQ's 2 FTE-year assumption), citing reusable ML/infrastructure investments not fully accounted for in the original framework.

Strategic decision: rather than disputing NVIDIA's lower estimate (which was substantively legitimate given their existing infra), proposal strategy pivoted to emphasize ongoing maintenance/tuning cost as the more commonly underestimated component of build decisions — an argument grounded in observed patterns rather than NVIDIA-specific assumptions, preserving credibility.

This reframing was later confirmed (via Rayhan's account of the internal decision meeting) to have meaningfully influenced the committee's deliberation, even though it did not change the final outcome.
